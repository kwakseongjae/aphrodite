About the Maker

Based in Seoul, I create furniture exclusively from solid walnut wood, celebrating its natural grain and warm character. Each piece is handcrafted using traditional joinery techniques combined with modern design sensibilities.

My practice focuses on creating heirloom-quality furniture that develops a rich patina over time. I work directly with clients to create pieces that harmonize with their living spaces while honoring the material itself.

The workshop is located in the Mapo district, where I've been refining my craft for over a decade. My training combines Korean woodworking traditions with European furniture making techniques.
