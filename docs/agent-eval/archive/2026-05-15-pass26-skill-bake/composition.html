About

We design the things that outlast the meeting where they were approved.

Method

Forma has maintained a single practice since 1961: the design of identity systems for institutions — corporations, municipalities, cultural organizations, and public agencies. We work in wordmarks, signage, environmental graphics, packaging, and typographic standards. We do not work in campaigns. A campaign expires. An identity accumulates.

Principles

Every mark we produce is drawn to function at one centimeter and at one hundred meters. We work in two colours because two colours are enough. We set type in Garamond because we have not found a reason to stop. We present our work in bound documents, not slide decks, because the client deserves something that sits on a shelf after the projector is turned off.
