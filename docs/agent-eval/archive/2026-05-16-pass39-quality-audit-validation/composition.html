Seoul Dining Table

Black walnut • 180 × 90 × 74 cm • 2024

A dining table commissioned for a private residence in Hannam-dong, featuring a live edge slab sourced from sustainable forests in Gangwon Province. The table showcases the natural beauty of walnut with minimal intervention, allowing the grain to guide the form.

Jeju Sideboard

Black walnut • 200 × 60 × 85 cm • 2023

Inspired by volcanic rock formations on Jeju Island, this sideboard features asymmetrical doors and a textured finish achieved through controlled burning of the wood surface. Each door is fitted with hand-carved walnut handles that follow the natural curvature of the grain.

Han River Chair

Black walnut • 65 × 55 × 75 cm • 2023

Designed for contemplative spaces near the Han River, this chair balances traditional joinery techniques with contemporary form. The seat is carved from a single piece of walnut, allowing for a continuous flow of grain from back to seat.

Gyeongbokgung Cabinet

Black walnut • 120 × 45 × 130 cm • 2022

A reinterpretation of traditional Korean cabinet forms, scaled for modern living spaces. The drawer fronts feature inlaid brass patterns inspired by palace roof tiles, while the interior is lined with handcrafted paper from Jeonju.

Bukchon Bed

Black walnut • 200 × 180 × 110 cm • 2022

Designed for a renovated hanok in Bukchon, this bed frame integrates traditional Korean architecture elements with contemporary comfort. The headboard features carved panels inspired by moon doors, creating a play of light and shadow in the bedroom.

Namsan Bookshelf

Black walnut • 120 × 30 × 200 cm • 2021

Organized around the concept of layers, this bookshelf features staggered shelves that create visual rhythm while accommodating books of varying sizes. The asymmetrical design is balanced through careful attention to negative space and proportion.
